## Key Families

- equation containers and display behavior: `equation`
- fractions and roots: `frac`, `root`, `sqrt`
- matrices and cases: `mat`, `vec`, `cases`
- attachments and delimiters: `attach`, scripts, `lr`, `mid`
- math group pages in `groups.yml`: variants, styles, sizes, roots, attach, `lr`

## Version Notes

- `0.14.0` added `frac.style`.
- `0.14.0` added `math.equation.alt` for accessibility-sensitive equations.
